## Common Questions

### 1. The game is not responding to my swipes.
Please make sure you are using the latest version of the app. If the problem continues, restart the app and try again.

### 2. I found a bug or unexpected behavior.
Please send us a description of the issue, your device model, iOS version, and, if possible, screenshots or a screen recording.

### 3. Ads are not loading or displaying correctly.
Ad availability may vary depending on device, network connection, region, and ad service availability.

### 4. How do I remove ads?
If the app includes a paid ad removal option, please make sure your purchase was completed successfully. If needed, contact support and include your device and purchase details.

### 5. The app language is not what I expected.
The app language may follow your device language settings or the in-app localization currently available.

## 常见问题

### 1. 游戏没有正确识别我的滑动操作。
请确认你使用的是最新版本的应用。如果问题仍然存在，请先关闭应用后重新打开，再进行尝试。

### 2. 我发现了 bug 或异常情况。
请尽量向我们提供问题描述、设备型号、iOS 版本，以及相关截图或录屏，这将帮助我们更快定位问题。

### 3. 广告没有正常加载或显示。
广告显示可能会受到设备、网络连接、地区以及广告服务状态的影响。

### 4. 如何去除广告？
如果应用提供付费去广告功能，请先确认购买是否成功完成。如仍有问题，请联系支持，并附上设备信息和购买相关说明。

### 5. 应用语言不是我预期的语言。
应用语言可能会根据你的设备语言设置，或当前应用已提供的本地化内容进行显示。
