About

Zhenyue is an Information Science Ph.D. student at Cornell. His research interest lies in Computational Social Science — an interdisciplinary area that studies human societies quantitatively using large volumes of data generated by human activities. Prior to joining Cornell, he earned his bachelor's and master's degrees from Nanjing University and worked as a full-time research assistant at Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business.
